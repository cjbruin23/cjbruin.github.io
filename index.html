Colin's Portfolio

Starter

I was born in Indianapolis, Indiana and spent the first 12 years of my life in the Indy area. After that, I moved to Zionsville, Indiana where I completed middle school and all of high school. Most of my childhood was centered around finding the most risky activities I could and dreamed of being a professional skateboarder and BMX biker. I had a solid 1.7 GPA by the end of my sophmore year. On a trip to California, a guy pulled up in a Lamborghini next to where I was standing so I asked him what he did. He said he started as an engineer and now ran his own company. When I asked him how to become an engineer he said study hard, so I put away the skateboard and bike and started picking up more books, finishing high school on a strong note.

College and Such

For college I decided to attend Indiana University and study economics and history. I enjoyed the first year and a half of my classes but then ran into problems. The economics classes were too theoretical and didn't have much practical knowledge. I decided to take a class in Econometrics, where statistical analysis is applied to quesitons in economics. We primarily used the program R, where our teacher stressed we learn how to program. I did not like the programing aspect as much but enjoyed statistics.

Tectonic Shift

I deceided to take a programming class in Python since Python was being rapidly developed to help users in statistical analysis and the like. I found enjoyment in the programming and logical reasoning that it took. I could spend hours trying to solve assigned and random problems that came up. I then shifted my focus from statistical analytics to programming and went to IUPUI where I got a certificate in web development. I worked a little bit on small web development projects until I found Kenzie and now, here I am.
